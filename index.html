How to add Slider?

Step 1. Go to Blogger Dashboard.

Step 2. Click Template.

Step 3. Then Edit HTML.

Step 4. Now Search for <!-- MasterSlider -->

Step 5. Edit Below Images. Example below:-

<img alt='' data-src='YOUR_IMAGE_LINK' src='' title=''/>

About me Widget Codes

Step 1. Go to Blogger Dashboard.

Step 2. Click Layout

Step 3. Edit About me Widget.

Step 4. Add below codes and Save it.

<p><img src="YOUR_PICTURE" /></p>

<p><strong>FEW_WORDS_ABOUT_YOU</strong> I’m a blogger...</p>
